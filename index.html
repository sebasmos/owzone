Generator and multifunctional ozonator
Caused by Covid-19

Owzone is a multifunctional ozone generator to desinfect indoor spaces.

With the goal of reducing the incidence and mortality generated by the COVID-19 outbreak in Colombia, we proposed a system of disinfection and external monitoring of the air of the patients' space in real time, fast and economical that implements a mobile application that allows record and visualize multiple variables of the environment.

What is the problem?

2021 IEEE HAC/SIGHT Projects - Focus on COVID-19 Response


Our project reduces the incidence and mortality generated by COVID-19 in Colombia by offering a system of disinfection and external monitoring of the air of the patients' space in real time, fast and economical that implements a dashboard, a platform that allows record and display multiple environmental and air variables, such as regeneration and purification time, and ozone concentration. Our solution is the OWZONE ozonator, which is a device that is at the TRL5 level, has a low cost and is easy to manufacture in Colombian territory. It is in the process of obtaining the permits and certifications for its respective distribution.
